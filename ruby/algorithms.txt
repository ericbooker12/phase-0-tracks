- Determine your floor destination and call it dest-floor.
- Determine your current floor and call it cur-floor.
- If dest-floor is greater than cur-floor:
    - press the up button.
    - otherwise press the down button.
- Wait for the elevator door to open.
- When the elevator door opens, enter the elevator.
- Press the number that corresponds the dest-floor.
- Wait for the elevator to got to dest-floor.
- When the door opens, exit the elevator.
- make cur-floor equal to dest-floor.
