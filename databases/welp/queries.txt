3. Join 3 tables:
SELECT users.firstName, users.lastName, businesses.name, reviews.stars, reviews.comment 
    FROM users
    JOIN reviews
    ON users.id = reviews.user_id
    JOIN businesses
    ON reviews.business_id = businesses.id;
